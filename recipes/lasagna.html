Lasagna

Description

Lasagne is an Italian dish consisting of layers of pasta interspersed with a tasty sauce, usually made with minced meat and tomatoes. The whole thing is topped with béchamel sauce and grated cheese before being baked au gratin. This generous, comforting dish has a melt-in-the-mouth texture and a rich flavour.

steps

  1. prepare the tomato sauce in a saucepan
  2. To do this, chop an onion very finely and fry in a few drops of olive oil. Add the quartered tomatoes, washed and peeled beforehand, with a little salt and pepper.
  3. Leave to simmer for around 2 hours over a low heat, covering part of the pan. Don't forget to stir occasionally and add stock as you go along.
