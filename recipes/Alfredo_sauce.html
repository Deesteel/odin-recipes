Alfredo sauce

Description

This Alfredo sauce recipe always turns out rich and creamy with lots of garlic and Parmesan flavor! A delicious way to transform plain fettuccine pasta, roast chicken, or steamed vegetables!

Ingrédients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
