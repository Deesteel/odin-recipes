Carbonara

Description

Savourez l’authentique carbonara italienne, une recette simple et gourmande sans crème !

Ingrédients

  • sel
  • pepper
  • egg yolk
  • 500g of pancetta
  • 50g of parmesan Reggiano
  • a tablespoon of olive oil
  • 300g of pasta

Steps

  1. Beat the egg yolks, adding 1 pinch of salt, 2 pinches of pepper and 40 g grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.
  2. Fill a large saucepan with water and heat with two pinches of coarse salt.
  3. Meanwhile, cut the pancetta into coarse strips and brown in a frying pan with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  4. When the water in the pan starts to boil, put in your pasta.
  5. Take two tablespoons of pasta cooking water, pour into the egg yolks and stir.
  6. Set the pancetta aside and keep the pan warm.
  7. Once the pasta is al dente, drain it briefly and put it in the frying pan, stirring.
  8. Pour the mixture into a bowl and fold in the egg yolk mixture.
  9. Add the pancetta and two pinches of pepper. Serve.
